Financial Aid

Recognizing financial challenges, our school assists families with a 12-month tuition installment plan, making education more accessible. This plan eases financial strain, ensuring that families can access education without facing overwhelming financial burdens, fostering inclusivity.

Grants

In our dedication to student success, we offer fee reductions to the top three achievers. Contact us for more information on this initiative showcasing our commitment to fostering excellence among our students.

Meal Programs

In addition to our school cafeteria, we offer subsidized meals through a monthly fee, ensuring both budgeting convenience and access to nutritious food for students. This optional service aims to support healthy eating choices while providing flexibility for families.
