About The Summer Program 2024

Parents want their children to have fun and learn over summer. So, our school set up a great 2024 summer program! Loads of courses are on offer, so students can pick what interests them most. It's all about having a fun and picking up new skills at the same time.


Check out the courses on the right! They're the summer programs for 2024 by our school. You can see how many students signed up for each course. If some of you want a course that's not listed, just tell us! We'd love to add it for you if the number of the group is enough. It's all about making this summer awesome for everyone!


Every course for this summer program has its own fee, but don't worry, they're all pretty affordable! These fees just cover the basics needed to make the program awesome. None of them are expensive, and we've made sure they're just enough to keep everything going smoothly.


To join a course, send us your full name, your email, and the course you want. Got another idea? Tell us! We'll discuss it with the parents, see if there's space, then add you. Simple! Just send us the details, and we'll make sure you're on the list for an awesome summer of learning and fun!
